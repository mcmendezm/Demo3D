Fascinating Insect Facts

Incredible Diversity

There are over 900,000 known species of insects, making up about 80% of the world's species.

Superhuman Strength

Ants can lift up to 50 times their own body weight, equivalent to a human lifting a truck.

Ancient Origins

Insects have been around for more than 400 million years, predating dinosaurs by over 200 million years.

Vital Pollinators

About 75% of all flowering plants rely on insects for pollination, including many of our food crops.
